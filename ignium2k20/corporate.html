Corporate Roadies 2.0



Corporate Roadies 2.0, an event that challenges one’s natural leadership and managerial instincts. Here’s your best chance to explore your business skills in the ‘Roadies’ spirit through fun activities. Put your potential to persuade and tackle stress to test.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1: Prise de Décision
Participants are required to take part individually. The participants are required to answer a questionnaire which will tell how good a manager you are because while leaders may bring us vision, inspiration and challenge, these things count for nothing without the efficient implementation brought about by good management.
Round 2: Monopoly
A team event where participants are required to showcase their abilities to manage finances in a completely new, multilevel version of monopoly. The team which can prove its domination in the market will be selected for the next round.
Round 3: Karyaneeti
Participants will be given a list of products that come from rural area or indigenous Indian product for which they need to prepare a marketing plan including branding and sales promotion strategies which will help this product to make a mark in the international market.
GUIDELINES:
  • Participants will take part individually but depending on the rounds they will be randomly grouped based on the task.
  • This event will take place in three rounds.
