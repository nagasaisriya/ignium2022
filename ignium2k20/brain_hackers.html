BRAIN HACKERS



Have you got the fundamentals of Electronics right? Who should win if Resistors and Capacitors fight? If you want to brainstorm answers to more of such mind-boggling questions, the technical fun-packed game, BRAIN HACKERS, is your go-to event. Here is where we scrutinize the Ohms, Faraday and Henry in You as you battle to be crowned the King of circuits. .

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all B.E/B.Tech and M.Tech students who are acquainted with electronics.

Round 1:
It comprises of a multiple choice based exam (pen and paper) testing the theoretical and practical applications of electronics. They are expected to answer the questions in the given time limit.
