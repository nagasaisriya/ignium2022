ABOUT US

Late Sri.G.Pulla Reddy, an eminent businessman of integrity and impeccable reputation, started the G.Narayanamma Institute of Technology & Science (GNITS) for Women in 1997. It is presently under the eminent guidance of Chairman Sri.P.Subba Reddy and Secretary Smt.Srividya Reddy Gunampalli.

For further details, please visit us at   www.gnits.ac.in


The regular activities conducted by the ISTE Student Chapter include National Level Paper Presentations, Poster Presentations, Project Exhibitions, Technical Quiz Contests, Seminars and Workshops.
