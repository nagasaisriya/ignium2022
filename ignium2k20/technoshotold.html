TECHNOSHOT



There's no denying. In today's day and age, Photography is the most sought after recreation. Are you an amateur photographer? A professional? Or someone ready with a camera everywhere you go? Hey there, we have news for you.

IGNIUM 2k18, gives you the opportunity to unleash the photog in you through an online Photography contest, Technoshot. Every picture has a story to tell. What have you got to share? Bring it on, techies.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The contest is open to everyone.

GUIDELINES:
  • Upload your *original* click to our Facebook page, by sending it to us as a message.
  • Add a description of your picture and let the world know your perspective.
  • Share it with the hashtags,#technoshot #ignium18 #gnits
  • The picture with the maximum number of likes and shares, wins the contest
Submission deadline:  The deadline to send us your images is 19 February 2018.
NOTE:
  • Only original photographs are to be uploaded.
  • The faster your submission, the better is your chance to get more shares and likes.
  • Judges may also select the images of their choice as the best, irrespective of the number of likes and shares, incase it is exceptionally good.
