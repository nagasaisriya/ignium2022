Rules



Problem Statement:
Design a manually controlled robot which is capable of maneuvering through tough terrain (includes sand, stones, gravel, inclinations etc.) in the least time, avoiding penalties.

SPECIFICATIONS

 • A Maximum of 3 members per team is allowed.
• Only 2 per team are allowed at a time of competition at the arena.
• Bot dimensions should not exceed 30cm*30cm*20cm. (5% tolerance is allowed).
• The bot can be wired or wireless.
• Readymade bots are not entertained.
• Wireless bots should have sufficient binding capacity.
• Wired bots are suggested to have at least 2 m long wires.
• The weight of bot should not exceed 5 kg. (10% tolerance is allowed)
• You can use standard chassis.
• Power supply should not exceed 12V.
• Robot must be waterproof. (The coordinators and organisers are not responsible for the damage caused to your robots.)

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA

 • Judgments will be time based i.e. those bots reaching the finishing line in minimum amount of time shall be the winners.
• Not more than 4 hand touches is allowed and a penalty of 5 seconds will be added for every hand touch.
• Total time will be the sum of actual time taken finish the path and penalties gained.
• A bot causing any type of damage to the arena will be disqualified.
• Pulling wires to handle the bot would result in disqualification.
• Same bot cannot be used by more than one team. If the members of the team participate again with different team name they need to use another bot.
• Arguments with the coordinators and organisers might lead to the disqualification of the bot. The decision of the Event Organizers will be final and abiding for all.
