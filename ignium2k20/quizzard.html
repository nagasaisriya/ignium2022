Quizzard



Are brilliant general awareness and spontaneity your strongest suits? Let being a know-it-all reap its fruits. Here’s a shout-out to every passionate quizzer out there. To find the ultimate IQ Wizard, IGNIUM 2K18 presents to you its biggest battle of brains, QUIZZARD.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The event is open to all.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1: The participants would be given a set of techno-general questions. They are expected to answer the given questions in the given time limit. Better the performance, higher are the chances of qualifying to the next round.
Round 2: In this round, a few audio and video clips will be played. The participants would be asked a few questions based on those clips. The top teams would be qualifying to the final round.
Round 3: This is a quiz round which would test the participants on their general awareness, agility and decision making through a set of fun-filled rounds.
GUIDELINES:
  • Maximum of two members per team.
  • This event will take place in three rounds.
