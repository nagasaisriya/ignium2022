ELECTRONIC MAZE



You sit there all day long, but did you explore it enough? IGNIUM 2K18 brings to all the ECE folks, ELECTRONIC MAZE, a treasure hunt of Electronics in your very own building. There is no better way to know, how well you know your department block and what you learn there.

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:

The participants should be well acquainted with technical knowledge related to electronics subjects and software.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1:
SCRAMBLED WORDS: The participants will be given Technical scrambled words and they have to find the answers of the questions provided and mark them properly in the box of scrambled words..
Round 2:
TECHNICAL HUNT: Every team will be provided with clues, which include a blend of technical and basic sciences concepts that are conceptually designed to test your intellect. As you proceed to the further rounds each level brings a spine-chilling experience with fascinating tasks.
GUIDELINES:
  • Maximum of 2 members per team.
  • The event will take place in 2 rounds.
