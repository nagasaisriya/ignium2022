CIRCUITUS CHALLENGE



Could deciphering circuits be a piece of cake? Did you ever gauge the CRO outputs you take? IGNIUM 2K18 brings to you the Circuit Challenge, your ultimate chance to put your circuit skills to test and showcase your technical prowess to the fullest.

EVENT DESCRIPTION:
Round 1:
The participants are shown an assembled circuit and they have to reproduce it on a sheet provided. The faster they reproduce, the better are their chances to qualify. .
Round 2:
The participants are now given a circuit on a sheet of paper and would be asked to implement the same using MULTISIM. The students with optimum results would be qualified for the next level.
Round 3:
After the simulation level, the qualified students would be given a circuit and asked to implement it on a bread board. The contestants should produce the desired result. Winners are declared based on how fast the participants produce the correct output.
