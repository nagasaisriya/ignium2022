Line Follower

follow me in black!!



“LINE FOLLOWER is a challenge to design self-controlled bots to follow black path in a typical curves and crosses over a white backend till the end."

Rules



Problem Statement:
Design an autonomous line following robot that is capable of navigating itself on the guided black line on a white background. Robot should complete the path in the least time possible.

SPECIFICATIONS OF PATH

 • A flex sheet of white color with designed black path is used for arena.
• Width of the black line is 3cm.(maximum tolerance of 1cm)
• Shape of arena may include right and acute angles, junctions, curves or circles.
• Color of the black line in the total arena may vary in small proportions.

SPECIFICATIONS OF TEAM AND BOT

 • A Maximum of 3 members per team is allowed.
• Bot dimensions should not exceed 25cm*25cm*20cm (l*b*h) (5% tolerance is allowed).
• Bots must be strictly autonomous.
• You can use standard chassis.
• Power supply must be strictly on the board and should not exceed 12V
• Readymade bots are not entertained.

JUDGEMENT CRITERIA

 • The robot should complete the path in the least time.
• Not more than 3 manual touches are allowed once the competition begins and a penalty of 5 seconds will be added for every touch.
• Total time will be the sum of actual time taken to finish the path and penalties gained.
• A bot causing any type of damage to the arena will be disqualified.
• Not more than 2 resets allowed if the bot moves out of arena at the time of beginning.
• Same bot cannot be used by more than one team. If the members of the team participate again with different team name they need to use another bot.
• Arguments with the coordinators and organizers might lead to the disqualification of the bot. The decision of the Event Organizers will be final and abiding for all.
