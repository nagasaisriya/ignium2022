ROBOTRON



Are you an avid buff of Robo challenges and fierce racing games? If yes, then here’s your best bet. IGNIUM 2K18 presents to you, its first ever edition of ROBOTRON, where your robot has to maneuver meticulously and race against hurdles and challenges. On your Mark! Are we game?

Rules



ELIGIBILITY:
The event is open to all B.E/B.TECH/M.TECH students.
EVENT DESCRIPTION:
A track, created by the Robotron team, will be provided. The robot is expected to travel along the track by crossing all the hurdles (without tripping) and without touching the sides of the track.
The evaluation will be based on:
  • Smooth movement of the robot
  • Ability to cross hurdles without tripping
  • Ability to finish the journey without touching the sides of the track
Any other attributes of the robot would fetch the team extra points.
The participants are expected to build their robots prior to the event.
TEAM SIZE: Maximum of four members per team
